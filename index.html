Portfolio

This is where you can explore some of the exciting projects I have worked on, ranging from data science and analytics to software development and more. Each project is unique and showcases my skills and expertise in different areas, as well as my ability to collaborate with others.

Jun 2020

IPA: Automated Test Scripts in Python

Automated test scripts for a medical software. The scripts were written in Python and used Selenium to automate the testing of the software. Test results are documented in a word document.

Jan 2022

Data Science Project: Rockfall Risk

My team and I used Data Science to predict the risk of death by rockfalls on a busy cantonal road in the town of Schiers GR, Switzerland. We learned the importance of data simulation and predictions.

Apr 2022

Personal Project: Image Editor

To edit product images for my website, I created a simple program in Python. The editor allows you to crop, resize and compress images. This is very important for my website because it's essential to have fast loading pages.

Jun 2022

Data Science Project: Influence of water temperature on coffee taste

My team and I received data from a professional coffee barista and analyzed the data to find out if there is a correlation between water temperatures and coffee tastes for specific machines and roasting techniques.

Jan 2023

Data Science Project: Tinder 4 Movies

The goal for this project was to create a movie recommendation system based on similar movies and user's preferences (contect based and collaborative systems). The system was built using Python and the data was scraped from the TMDB API and MovieLens database.

Jun 2023

Data Science Project: Policy Advice on Energy Production without Greenhouse Gas Emissions

My team and I used Data Science to analyze the energy production in Switzerland and other surrounding countries and give policy advice on how to produce energy without greenhouse gas emissions.
